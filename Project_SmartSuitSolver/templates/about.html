About Smart Suit Solver

Smart Suit Solver is an AI-powered platform that helps legal professionals analyze case data and predict potential outcomes with high accuracy. Our mission is to streamline case strategy, reduce manual research time, and provide actionable insights that guide lawyers toward better decisions.

What We Do

  • Case Outcome Prediction: We use machine learning algorithms trained on historical case data (FIRs, judgments, precedents) to forecast possible results.
  • Document Analysis: Automatic parsing of legal documents (FIRs, charge sheets, judgments) to extract key facts, timelines, and legal provisions.
  • “What-If” Simulator: Compare different legal strategies and see how changing facts or arguments may affect predicted outcomes.
  • Legal Risk Meter: A composite score indicating time, emotional, and cost risk for each litigant or scenario.

Lawyer Learning Panel

Empower junior lawyers and interns to build real-world skills while contributing to meaningful legal work. Our Lawyer Learning Panel offers:

  • Simulated cases to practice drafting and research
  • Earn “XP” points for providing legal drafts (e.g., charges, bail pleas)
  • All submissions verified by experienced senior lawyers
  • Opportunities to take on low-cost or pro-bono cases for social good

What’s Coming Next

  • Interactive Dashboard: A visual interface showing case-win probabilities over time, filtered by jurisdiction, judge, or type of crime.
  • Expanded Document Types: Soon, you’ll be able to upload Charge Sheets, Judgments, and Written Advices, not just FIRs.
  • Collaborative Tools: Share insights with your legal team, annotate documents together, and export summary reports in PDF/ Word.
  • API Access: Integrate Case Outcome Prediction into your existing legal-tech solutions (e.g., case-management software).
